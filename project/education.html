My Education

Primary Education

I went to El-Fouad International School for primary, and my favorite subject was science. I took part in a singing and dancing competition, which was a fun experience. My proudest achievement was acing every term. People saw me as smart but not very sociable, and I was shorter than most of my classmates.

Secondary Education

At Al-Alson International School, I continued to enjoy science as my favorite subject. I participated in a math competition and learned about manipulation by using friends as pawns. Unfortunately, I faced challenges with fake friends and only had mid-tier relationships with my teachers, making my experience a mix of lessons and struggles.

Online Courses

I've taken online courses in C++ programming, CS50x, PowerPoint, Excel, and Word. I'm focused on Software Engineering and Computer Science to become a great software engineer. I prefer learning through videos and interactive activities, making platforms like EdX and Udemy my top choices.
